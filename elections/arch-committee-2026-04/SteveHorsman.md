# Steve Horsman's Election pitch

Name: Steve Horsman, [@stevenhorsman](https://github.com/stevenhorsman)

## Candidacy

### Who I am

I am a software engineer at IBM, based in the UK, where I work on Confidential Computing based on Z mainframe (s390x) technology. I've been involved in open source for many years, contributing to various projects.

#### Why you might know me

I've been involved in the Kata Containers project since July 2021, when development in Confidential Containers started. Over the past three years, I have served on the Architecture Committee and have been an active contributor to the project.

You might have seen me:

- Reviewing PRs and helping with code reviews across the codebase
- Working on CI/CD improvements and test infrastructure
- Contributing to Kata Containers releases
- Participating in community calls and discussions
- Working on security improvements and vulnerability management

#### Why you might want to elect me

I'm committed to the long-term health and sustainability of the Kata Containers project and working hard
to help with this.

My focus areas align with making the project more robust and secure. If elected I plan to continue to
focus on security and quality, and improving our CI/CD, keeping dependencies up-to-date, addressing our
CVE backlog and improving our security scanning.

#### Why you might want to elect someone else

I have served on the Architecture Committee for three years, making me one of the longest-standing members. While this brings continuity , you might prefer fresh perspectives to drive the next phase of the community's growth.

If you are bored with hearing my voice in the Kata community calls!

Realistically, although I feel I can provide value to the community doing some of the admin and "donkey work",
much of the community has far more knowledge and experience of the virtualization technologies that Kata Containers
is built on than I do.
